About the Seventh-day Adventists

It all started when there was a Crusade held at Shimizu grounds at Fred Joe in 2004, where a majority number of youths where baptized. Then the Branch was formed under the leadership of Elder Tyson Halumba as its first Branch Leader. After one (1) year of being a Branch, in 2005 it become a Company named Katimamulilo Company. The first Company leader was Elder Tyson Halumba. After nominations in November, 2006 the next set of leadership was ushered in January 2006 under the leadership of Elder Webby Chama as its leader. Just after one (1) month on 26th February, 2006 Katimamulilo Company become a Church standing independent now from Garden Main Church and Elder Webby Chama was the First Elder of KATIMAMULILO SDA CHURCH with his team of Elders namely; Elder Mwiinga Collins, Elder Mwenya Derrick and Elder Mahemu Oliver. The First Church Treasurer was Brenda Munahimuchimpa The First Church Clerk was Elder Cooper Chongo The First Head Deacon was Deacon Cosmas Siampeyo The First Head Deaconess was Lilian Siachami Zimba. All this time the Church was worshiping at Ngwelele Primary School. The Church bought its plot and moved to its plot in the Summer of 2008. The Church was engaged in a legal battle from 2012 when It was sued in the High Court of Zambia for trespass Sr Mushipe Zebedee and the matter has since been deliberated and only waiting for Judgment. This has been a brief History of the Church.
